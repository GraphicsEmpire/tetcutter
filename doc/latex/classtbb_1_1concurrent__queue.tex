\subsubsection*{template$<$typename T$>$class tbb\+::concurrent\+\_\+queue$<$ T $>$}

\hyperlink{structA}{A} high-\/performance thread-\/safe queue. 

Multiple threads may each push and pop concurrently. Assignment and copy construction are not allowed.
